Travel

Book early for Labor Day weekend!

In 2025, Seattle ranked as the #1 USA domestic travel destination over Labor Day weekend. Book your tickets early. SEA airport will be busy and hotels will fill quickly.

Here are our recommendations:

Airbnb

Since our venue is tucked away from the city, we recommend checking out Airbnb. There are several large options in Renton, Maple Valley and Covington close to our venue.

Hyatt Regency Lake Washington at Seattle's Southport

1053 Lake Washington Boulevard North, Renton, WA 98056. This polished hotel sits right in front of Gene Coulon Memorial Beach Park and is within walking distance of restaurants and entertainment at The Landing.

Embassy Suites by Hilton Seattle Tacoma International Airport

15920 West Valley Highway, Seattle, WA 98188. Great for comfort and convenience. This hotel is within walking distance of Westfield Southcenter.
